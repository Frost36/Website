About Me

Currently

Hi. This is me. I'm a graduate (2014) of Carnegie Mellon University with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and a minor in Mathematical Sciences. I'm currently working at Amazon, in Seattle, WA, on the SCOT Topology team. In senior year of college I worked on a senior thesis with Karl Crary at Carnegie Mellon, specifically working on a methodology for verifying the correctness of a compiler phase by using a combined language, which you can read more about on the Projects page.

Interests

I have a passion for building things, be it software, computers, or constructions of folded paper. Among other things, I'm interested in algorithms, programming languages, compilers, and back-end software engineering. I also quite enjoy mathematics, logic, and philosophy.
